## Releases

Each Carbon Design System release includes package releases that are
independently versioned following the industry-standard
[semantic versioning](https://semver.org) specification.

The Carbon team also ships package minor releases every other week and when
necessary, patch releases between minor versions to address bugs. Read more in
Carbon's
[release radar](https://github.com/carbon-design-system/carbon/wiki/Release-radar).

### Carbon v10

Carbon v10 is the digital expression of the IBM Design Language, v2. The updated
components required some product redesigns, however they had a clearly
identifiable IBMness and created a connective tissue between all product
experiences.

### Carbon v11

Carbon v11 uses newer browser capabilities and includes quality of life updates
for improved designer / developer experiences. As v2 of the IBM Design Language
is still current, Carbon v11 will not require brand-driven product redesigns.
Read more about the upcoming
[Carbon v11 release](https://medium.com/carbondesign/whats-coming-to-carbon-in-2021-39a4c7d1762a).
