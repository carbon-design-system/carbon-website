## Release schedule

Each Carbon Design System release includes the releases of the libraries that
are primarily maintained by the Carbon team. Those libraries are independently
versioned following the industry-standard
[semantic versioning](https://semver.org) specification.

Where the Carbon team strives for frictionless major version updates through
release documentation, migration tooling, etc., Carbon 10 was a unique release
as it was the first to implement the updated IBM Design Language.

Unlike Carbon 10, the upcoming Carbon 11 release will not require you to
redesign your product as the IBM Design Language version has not changed since
Carbon 10. Read more about the upcoming
[Carbon 11 release](https://medium.com/carbondesign/whats-coming-to-carbon-in-2021-39a4c7d1762a).
